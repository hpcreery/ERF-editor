Line Unification

Line Unification (LU) is a read-write cleanup action. It is designed to reduce the number of lines in a layer by eliminating redundant lines (replace pairs of lines with single lines wherever possible), without changing the design of the layer. The system preserves the correct overlap between touching features in order to avoid the generation of slivers.

LU operates on shapes of any layer. The associated drill and rout layers influence results of this action for board layers.
