Function

 Reports Lines features which are fully exposed by SM
Note: Copper Lines features usually are not fully exposed by SM opening, so if such case exists it could point on a potential problem.
Note: A fully embedded line, or a line that is a part of drawn area, will not be reported in this category.
In the situation where a line is leaving a Pad or a drawn area and is fully exposed by SM, only that part of the line that is not embedded in the Pad or the drawn area will be reported.<>

Activation

Pads Checks

Known Limitations
 Lines with the attributes .pattern_fill, .sliver_fill, and .nomenclature will be ignored.
