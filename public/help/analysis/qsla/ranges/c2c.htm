Purpose

    Two close circuits may bridge during imaging, etching, plating or soldering, and result in a direct short.

 

Function

Reports unconnected circuit elements in the same layer that are closer than the action parameter pp_spacing . Measurement are spacing segments, provided the segment only touches copper at its end points. If a job was created directly from a CAD database, extra functionality is provided. A measurement between two circuits of the same CAD net, even if unconnected in the same layer, will be placed in the CAD Self Spacing category rather than in Circuit to Circuit.
