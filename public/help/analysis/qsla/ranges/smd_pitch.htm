Purpose

Provides a review of the pitch sizes used in rows of SMD pads. It can be used as a measure of the difficulty in producing the job.

Function

Reports first measurement of parallel evenly spaced SMD pads for each row in layer.

Activation

 

Known Limitations

·    Pads whose dimensions (width and height) differ by more than 5 mil will not be considered as belonging to the same row. If the narrow dimension (width for horizontal rows) of two adjacent pads differs by more than 1 mil, they will not be considered as belonging to the same row.

·    Evenly spaced pads belonging to different discrete SMD components may be considered to belong to the same row of one package.
