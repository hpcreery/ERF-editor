Purpose

 To ensure that the drill which pierces the outer layer is exposed by the corresponding solder mask.
Note: Category name is dynamic,depending on the drill type involved in the check.

Function

 Reports drills which are covered by the solder mask (there are no shapes covering the drill in the corresponding SM layer).

Activation

Covered Drills check in SM Test List is selected and the drill layer pierces (at least one) outer layer.
