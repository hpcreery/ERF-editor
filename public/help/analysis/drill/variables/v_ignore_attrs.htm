ERF Variable

Description

v_ignore_attrs

If a drill hole is assigned at least one attribute specified in a user-defined list, the drill hole is ignored by the action. Currently, the variable supports the . drill and .via_type attributes.
If v_ignore_attrs is defined as .drill=plated, results may be generated for the PTH Summary category. Holes listed in this category do not possess the .drill attribute, so they are not filtered out by the v_ignore_attrs variable.
