Purpose

This category is more of a warning. Sometimes a hole is omitted by a mistake,

and then two pads will be located on both sides of a stack without a connecting hole.

Function

Reports pads in the top and bottom layers of the drilled stack of the inspected drill layer where all of the following apply:

*    No drill touches the top pad

*    and both pads are non SMD

*    and the distance between the pad centers is no more than the ERF variable duph_tolerance.

Measurement is a point; the location of the top pad center.
