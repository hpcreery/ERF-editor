Function

Reports pad-to-pad spacing measurements that are less than the upper limit of the green value of this category

(as defined in the ERF file, and not as specified in the pp_spacing parameter) when one of the Pads has an

.smd attribute and the other does not.

(When the two Pads have an .smd attribute, this spacing is reported in the SMD to SMD (Spacing)

(smd2smd) category.

 

Activation

When the ERF variable classify_pad2pad = yes.

Known Limitations

None
