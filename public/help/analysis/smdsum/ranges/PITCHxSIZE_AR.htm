Function

Reports (per size/pitch combination) the annular ring of the solder mask around the SMD pads.

Measurements are segments describing the minimal distance between the outlines of the pad and the outline of the solder mask clearance.

 

 

Activation

When the ERF variable v_sort_by_pitch = Yes .

Known Limitations

Gasketing (solder mask clearance smaller than SMD pad) is reported as zero annular ring.
